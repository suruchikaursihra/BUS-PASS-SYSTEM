DTC introduces Computerized Bus Pass System Download Computerized Bus Pass Application Form You may get DTC Bus Pass Know about Pass Sections Do you know the procedure for obtaining a Bus Pass? DTC introduces Computerized Bus Pass System DTC launches in house computerization of Bus Passes from 1st July, 2007. 39 Bus Pass Centers computerized and one pass center is functioning manual all over Delhi. Bus Passes shall be issued by computers within 2 minutes. Each Bus Pass holder shall be photographed through a web camera and issued a ID card cum bus pass. Rs. 15 towards cost of ID Card-cum-Bus Pass shall be payable for issuing the Photo ID Card-cum-Bus Pass. Bus Pass shall be issued for 1-2-3-4-5-6 (barring Senior Citizens who opt. upto 12 months) as per choice of the applicant except student concession destination pass which will be issued on month basis only. Passes to Freedom Fighters persons with disabilities shall be issued on yearly basis. No security is required to deposit. No requirement of carrying photograph with application. Bus Passes can be renewed during the last 3 days of the expiry of Bus Pass No change in existing Bus Pass fare structure.
